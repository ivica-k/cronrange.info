  • Cron examples
    Standard cron expressions
    */5 * * * *
    - every five minutes
    15 14 1 * *
    - at 14:15 every 1st of the month
    0 0 1,15 * *
    - every 1st and 15th at 00:00
    AWS EventBridge expressions
    0 18 ? * MON-FRI *
    - at 18:00 between days Monday and Friday
    0/50 8-17 ? * THU-FRI *
    - at minute 50 between hours 08:00-17:00 between days Thursday and Friday

Have you ever wondered when will your cron job be triggered, or how many times?

Cron Range allows you to easily predict up to 100 dates and times of your cron job executions. Now with support for AWS EventBridge expressions!

Choose a starting datetime, enter the cron expression and pick the number of predictions you would like to see.
